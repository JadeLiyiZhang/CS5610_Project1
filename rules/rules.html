Battleship Rules

Welcome Aboard, Captain! As the captain of a powerful naval fleet, your mission is to outsmart the enemy and sink their ships before they decimate your own. Strategic planning and a sharp mind will serve as your best tools in this high-seas confrontation.

  • Arrange Your Ships: Place your ships on a 10x10 grid. Your fleet ranges from the nimble Destroyer (2 squares) to the mighty Aircraft Carrier (5 squares). Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically but must not overlap or extend off the grid.
  • Fire at Will: Take turns with your opponent calling out grid coordinates (e.g., B6, H9) to attack. Announce "hit" for successful strikes and "miss" for failed attempts.
  • Track Your Battles: Mark hits and misses on your grid to refine your strategy and deduce the enemy's ship locations.
  • Report Ship Sinkings: When all parts of a ship are hit, declare it sunk.
  • Win the War: The first to send all enemy ships to the depths wins.
